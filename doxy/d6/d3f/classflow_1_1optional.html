Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class flow::optional< T >

Manages an optionally contained value, one that may or may not be present.

This class functions as a stripped down version of the soon to exist std::optional. Once std::optional is released and implemented by major compilers, this class will be aliased to be std::optional instead, and it will serve as a drop in replacement assuming the standard does not change.
